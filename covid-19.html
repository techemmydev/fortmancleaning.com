DISINFECTIONS & SANITATIONS

FCL is offering disinfections and sanitations for your office, medical centre or place of work. Using the more sophisticated electrostatic spray technology and proved products to guaranty your business is safe to stay. It uses a product Zono that provides cost-effective, residual polymer-based, broad-spectrum antimicrobial protection, lasting up to 30 days on any threatened surface. Zono, when applied to a surface by spraying, wiping or ‘fogging’; leaves behind a mono-molecular layer that permanently bonds to the surface and the more important for us: our products are colourless, odourless, non-leaching, environmentally safe, non-corrosive and whilst completely gentle for humans, it is water-based, alcohol-free, non-toxic and provides unique, proven, long-lasting protection.
